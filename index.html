About Our Company

Spearheading India's deep space initiatives and advancing human spaceflight research, and enable technical excellence while enabling a skilled, reliable workforce.

We are Spica Space Academy, India's premier institution dedicated to Astronautics Research and Exploration. We specialize in providing a vital Astronaut Training Overview and hands-on technical programs. We are dedicated experts in crafting bold research, developing distinctive talent, and building an enduring workforce development pipeline for the nation's ambitious space sector.
